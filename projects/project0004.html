UNFINISHEDNESS AND THE OPEN PLAN

ARTCENTER CDMX: A 600,000 SQFT VERTICAL CAMPUS IN THE HEART OF MEXICO CITY. A CITY WHERE ONLY 10% OF FORMAL ARCHITECTURE IS DESIGNED BY ARCHITECTS, WITH THE INFORMAL OFTEN EQUALLY COMPELLING.

WITHOUT ATTEMPTING TO IMITATE OR EMULATE INFORMAL ARCHITECTURE OR TO ROMANTICIZE THE CONDITIONS THAT NECESSITATE ITS IMPLEMENTATION, THIS PROJECT SEEKS TO QUESTION THE CONDITION OF COMPLETION WITHIN ARCHITECTURE AND EXPLORE THE POTENTIAL OF UNFINISHEDNESS.
